About Gegest Hotel

We Provide A Place Worth Spending Your Time In

Set in Theth, 2 km from Theth National Park, Gegest Hotel offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. Featuring a bar, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, all rooms include a balcony with a mountain view. At Gegest Hotel all rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels.
